PECI Origin Story

Art Direction, UX, + Design

Site created to tell the origin story of PECI, formerly known as Portland Energy Conservation Inc. Work on the origin site started as part of a storytelling taskforce. The site was to be launched for PECI's 45th anniversary, but the company was acquired by CLEAResult before our launch date. We worked dilligently to make sure the site was live before the official acquisition date and it was used to celebrate PECI's legacy. To this day, the PECI still uses the site to share its significant history.

Visit origin.peci.org.
